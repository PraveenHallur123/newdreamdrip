Accessibility Statement

Committed to providing an inclusive and accessible experience for all

Our Commitment

DreamDrip is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to make our website as accessible as possible.

We believe that fashion should be accessible to everyone, and we strive to create an inclusive online shopping experience.

Known Limitations

While we strive to make DreamDrip fully accessible, we are aware of some limitations:

Third-Party Content

Some third-party integrations (social media widgets, payment processors) may not be fully accessible. We are working with these providers to improve accessibility.

In Progress

Complex Visualizations

Our design customizer includes complex visual elements that may present challenges for some users. We provide alternative ways to customize designs.

Partial Workaround

Legacy Content

Some older content may not meet current accessibility standards. We are continually reviewing and updating our content.
