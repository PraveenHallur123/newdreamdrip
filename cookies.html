Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Understanding how you use our website
  • Personalizing your shopping experience
  • Improving our services

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Purpose: Necessary for the website to function

Examples: Shopping cart functionality, user authentication, security

Duration: Session or persistent

Cannot be disabled

Preference Cookies

Purpose: Remember your choices and preferences

Examples: Language settings, design customizations, size preferences

Duration: Persistent (up to 1 year)

Optional

Analytics Cookies

Purpose: Understand how visitors use our website

Examples: Page views, time on site, popular designs

Duration: Persistent (up to 2 years)

Optional

Marketing Cookies

Purpose: Deliver relevant advertisements

Examples: Retargeting ads, social media integration

Duration: Persistent (up to 1 year)

Optional
