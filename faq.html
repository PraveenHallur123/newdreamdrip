Frequently Asked Questions

Customization Questions

How does the customization process work?

Our customization process has 4 simple steps:

  1. Design: Use our interactive customizer to select colors, patterns, sleeves, length, and belt style
  2. Measurements: Enter your precise body measurements for a perfect fit
  3. Review: We'll send you a digital mockup for approval before production
  4. Creation: Our expert tailors handcraft your garment based on your specifications

Can I customize a pre-designed dress?

Absolutely! All designs in our shop can be customized. You can:

  • Change colors and fabrics
  • Modify sleeve styles and length
  • Adjust neckline and silhouette
  • Add or remove embellishments
  • Personalize with custom embroidery

Simply click "Customize" on any design page to begin.

How many times can I modify my design before ordering?

You can modify your design as many times as you like before placing your order. All changes are saved in real-time, and you can return to your design later.

Once you approve the final digital mockup and place your order, changes may incur additional fees depending on the production stage.

Rich textures for special occasions

Breathable fabrics for daywear

Measurement Questions

How do I take accurate measurements?

For best results:

  • Use a soft measuring tape
  • Stand naturally with feet together
  • Wear form-fitting undergarments
  • Have someone assist you if possible
  • Keep the tape parallel to the floor
  • Don't pull too tight - tape should be snug but not compressing

We provide detailed guides with pictures for each measurement.

What if my measurements change after ordering?

Contact us immediately if your measurements change:

  • Within 24 hours: Changes are free
  • After 24 hours but before production: Minor changes may be accommodated
  • During production: Changes may require restarting and additional fees

All custom garments include standard seam allowances for minor adjustments.

Do you offer virtual fitting consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary virtual fitting consultations for orders over $500. Our specialists will:

  • Guide you through the measurement process
  • Answer fit-related questions
  • Provide style recommendations based on your body type
  • Review your custom design for optimal fit

Contact us to schedule a consultation.

Shipping & Delivery Questions

How long does custom production take?

Production times vary based on complexity:

  • Standard custom orders: 4-6 weeks
  • Complex designs (embroidery, beading): 6-8 weeks
  • Bridal gowns: 8-12 weeks
  • Rush production: 2-3 weeks (additional fee applies)

You'll receive regular updates throughout the process.

What shipping carriers do you use?

We work with premium carriers to ensure safe delivery:

  • Domestic (US): FedEx Express, UPS 2nd Day Air
  • International: DHL Express, FedEx International Priority
  • Same-day delivery: Available in select metro areas

All garments are shipped in protective garment bags within sturdy boxes.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 5-10 business days.

Please note:

  • International customers are responsible for customs duties and taxes
  • Shipping costs are calculated at checkout
  • Delivery times may vary due to customs processing

Returns & Alterations Questions

What is your return policy for custom items?

Due to their personalized nature, custom-made garments are final sale and cannot be returned.

However, we offer:

  • Free alterations within 30 days of delivery if the garment doesn't fit based on your provided measurements
  • Repair or replacement for manufacturing defects
  • Store credit in exceptional circumstances

What about pre-designed items?

Pre-designed, non-custom items may be returned within 14 days of delivery if:

  • The item is unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • All tags are attached
  • It's returned in the original packaging

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

How do alterations work?

Our alteration process:

  1. Contact us within 30 days of delivery
  2. We'll provide a prepaid shipping label
  3. Ship the garment back to us
  4. Our tailors make the adjustments (typically 1-2 weeks)
  5. We ship it back to you at no extra cost

Alterations are free for fit issues based on your measurements. Style changes may incur fees.

Care & Maintenance Questions

How should I care for my DreamDrip garment?

Each garment comes with specific care instructions. General guidelines:

  • Dry cleaning: Recommended for most luxury fabrics
  • Hand washing: Gentle cycle with cold water for some fabrics
  • Storage: Use breathable garment bags, avoid plastic
  • Steaming: Preferred over ironing for delicate fabrics

Always check the care label specific to your garment.

Do you offer professional cleaning services?

Yes! We partner with premium dry cleaners nationwide. You can:

  • Use our prepaid cleaning mailer service
  • Drop off at any partner location
  • Schedule pick-up in select cities

Partner cleaners are specially trained to handle our luxury fabrics.
