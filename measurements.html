Measurement Guide

How to Measure

Use a soft measuring tape. Stand naturally with arms at your sides. Wear form-fitting clothing for accuracy.

Height

Stand straight against a wall without shoes. Mark the top of your head and measure from floor to mark.

Bust/Waist/Hip

Keep tape parallel to floor. Don't pull too tight - tape should be snug but not compressing.

Perfect Fit Guarantee

DreamDrip uses your measurements to create patterns specifically for your body. We guarantee a perfect fit or free alterations.
