Custom Orders Policy

No Returns

Custom-made garments are created specifically for you and cannot be returned or exchanged.

Free Alterations

We offer free alterations within 30 days of delivery if the garment doesn't fit as expected based on your provided measurements.

Quality Guarantee

If there are any manufacturing defects, we will repair or replace the garment at no cost.

Alteration Process

  1. Contact us within 30 days of delivery
  2. Describe the fit issue and include photos if possible
  3. We'll email you a prepaid shipping label
  4. Ship the garment back to us
  5. Our tailors make the adjustments (1-2 weeks)
  6. We ship it back to you at no extra cost

Damage & Defects

Manufacturing Defects

If your item arrives with a manufacturing defect:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Provide clear photos of the issue
  • We'll provide a prepaid return label
  • We'll repair or replace the item at no cost
  • We'll cover all shipping costs

Shipping Damage

If your item is damaged during shipping:

  • Document the damage with photos
  • Save all packaging materials
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • We'll file a claim with the carrier
  • We'll send a replacement immediately
